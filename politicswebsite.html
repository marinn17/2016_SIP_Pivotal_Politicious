Our mission

We are three high school girls who got involved in computer science after attending the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in the summer of 2016. Our mission is to provide an accessible way for people to learn about the politics and laws that affect their everyday lives, without any bias or hidden political influence. Politics isn’t just for tycoons in Washington— it starts with people like us, and it’s time for us to be heard.
